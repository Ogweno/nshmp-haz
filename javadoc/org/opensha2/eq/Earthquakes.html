org.opensha2.eq

Class Earthquakes

  • java.lang.Object
    • org.opensha2.eq.Earthquakes

  • public final class Earthquakes
extends Object
    Constants and utility methods pertaining to properties of earthquakes.

    Note: The various fields and methods of this class that relate to earthquake depths follow the positive-down convention of seismology.

    Author:
    Peter Powers
