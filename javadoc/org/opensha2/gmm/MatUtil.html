        public static double[] calc(Gmm model,
            Imt imt,

        Compute the median ground motion and its standard deviation for a specified model, intensity measure type (Imt), and source

        enum types are identified in matlab as e.g. Gmm$ASK_14 .

        Parameters:
        model - to use
        imt - intensity measure type (e.g. PGA, SA1P00)
        source - parameterization
        Returns:
        a two-element double[] containing the natural log of the median ground motion and its standard deviation

        Compute a spectrum of ground motions and their standard deviations for a specified model and source. All spectral periods supported by the model are returned.

        enum types are identified in matlab as e.g. Gmm$ASK_14 .

        Parameters:
        model - to use
        source - parameterization
        Returns:
        a MatSpectrum data container
